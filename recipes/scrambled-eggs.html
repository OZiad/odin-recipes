Scrambled Eggs

Creamy Cottage Cheese Recipe

Eggs are good! They're delicious and full of protein! EAT THEM

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
    • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup cottage cheese
  • ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh chives (optional)

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Pour beaten eggs into the skillet; let cook undisturbed until the bottom of the eggs begin to firm, 1 to 2 minutes.
  2. Stir cottage cheese and chives into eggs and season with black pepper. Cook and stir until eggs are nearly set, 3 to 4 minutes more.
